Humidity comparison

This graph showcases the relationship of humidity (%) to earth's latitude.The results appear to be somewhat random for the latitude versus humidity comparison. High humidity is prevelant along every latitude point. Low humidity does seem to be more noticeable between the +/- 20 to 40 degree latitude range but not consistent enough to say the latitude is the determining factor.
